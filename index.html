Welcome to my website! I'm Yinpu Li. I completed my Ph.D in Statistics at the Florida State University, under the direction of Professor Antonio R. Linero and Professor Sinha Deb. I received my MS in Statistics from the George Washington University and my BS in Mathematics Statistics from the East China Normal University. Presently, I'm serving as a Senior Data Scientist at Chewy, based in Washington. My work primarily revolves around the exciting realms of anomaly detection for SKU (products featured on our website) performance and the application of LLMs and multi-modal models for effective product matching. Before my tenure at Chewy, I had the opportunity to contribute as a Research Scientist at Convoy, where I focused on the development of efficient matching mechanisms. While my professional journey continues to unfold, my engagement in academia remains steadfast. My research aspirations span the diverse landscape of versatile Bayesian methods and the exploration of a wide array of Machine Learning (ML) and Artificial Intelligence (AI) techniques.
